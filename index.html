The Easy Animator

This application was a part of our object-oriented programming course and focused on creating an animator that had multiple views which rendered the animation in either a textual or visual format. My partner and I also included customizable options which allowed the user to manipulate the speed, start/stop point, and other aspects of the animation.

Text-based Adventure Game

Remember those old days of browser-based Dungeons and Dragons? Our final project for the intensive foundations in python course was exactly that! Employing object-oriented principles, I created a text-based adevnture game that had various puzzles and monsters that the user encountered!

iRes Application

Leading a team of 4 other individuals, I designed and assisted in implementing an application that helped centralize student life issues into one application. A student would submit an issue reuqest and their RA/CA would then become the liason between them and the respective department admin. Thus, making it easier for students to report issues and RAs/CAs to know what is going on with their students.
